Uncertainty Metrics Lab

Hands-on lab exploring practical uncertainty signals for multiclass classification: confidence, entropy and ensemble disagreement — plus an operational decision policy.

GitHub Repo Open Notebook

Overview

The goal is to understand how uncertainty behaves across predictions and how to convert it into simple decisions: automate, guide the user, or escalate for human review.

ML Reliability Uncertainty Jupyter Decision Policy

What’s inside

  • Top-1 confidence analysis and failure modes.
  • Normalized Shannon entropy scaled to [0,1].
  • Ensemble variability via disagreement / std signals.
  • Policy: AUTO / GRAY_ZONE / ESCALATE / GUIDE.

Decision policy (operational)

AUTO

High confidence + low entropy + stable ensemble → run automatically.

GRAY ZONE

Medium uncertainty → show options, add clarification prompts, monitor.

ESCALATE

High entropy or high disagreement → send to human review.

GUIDE

Low confidence but consistent pattern → ask targeted questions to disambiguate.
